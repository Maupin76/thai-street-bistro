Our Story

Chef Siri

Siri is a seasoned Thai cook with a lifelong passion for creating delicious and authentic dishes. She first discovered her love for cooking at the age of 10 and has been honing her skills ever since. Thai Street Bistro by Siri continues that tradition with fresh food, bold seasoning, and authentic Thai flavor.

Fresh Thai Food

Our food is made fresh to order with the flavors of Thailand. From Pad Thai and curry to Pad Krapow and Drunken Noodles, every dish is prepared with care and served with warm hospitality.
